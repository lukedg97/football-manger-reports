Assessment

A Linker type with an Engine's work ethic underneath, Danso's profile clusters around the central playmaker family — his best in-possession fit is Midfield Playmaker (12.15), with Deep-Lying Playmaker and Advanced Playmaker both scoring 12.03. The foundation is solid: Decisions 13, Composure 13, and Off The Ball 13 give him the mental toolkit to operate as a link in midfield, and Determination 14 with Work Rate 13 means he won't disappear from games. What caps his playmaker ceiling is Vision 11 and Passing 12 — functional, but short of the 14–15 range you'd want to truly run a team through him. The Box-to-Box Playmaker (11.97) and Central Midfielder (11.75) round out viable IP options, and taken together they paint a player who can contribute to build-up but is unlikely to be the primary creative force.

Out of possession he's arguably more useful than the IP scores suggest: Pressing Defensive Midfielder, Pressing Full-Back, and Pressing Wing-Back all land at 12.33 — his highest scores across the whole profile — driven by Aggression 13 and Work Rate 13. That pressing engine is the sharpest tool in the kit. What he cannot do is anchor defensively; Tackling 9, Strength 9, and Concentration 8 collectively close off any Anchor or ball-winning midfielder role, and Bravery 8 limits his Presser ceiling too. At 25, there's limited upside to bank on — what's here is broadly the player you're getting. He's a serviceable Midfield Playmaker or Pressing DM at this level, most useful in a system that values pressing and recycling rather than one demanding genuine creative leadership.
