An Engine / Runner type, and at this stage Heitzer’s usable FM26 value is clearest as a Full-Back (Defensive), where he scores 10.05, with Wing-Back (Defensive) close behind at 9.80 and Advanced Wing-Back (Defensive) at 9.72. The athletic and mentality base is the reason those roles surface: Acceleration 14, Pace 12, Stamina 12, Work Rate 12 and Determination 16 give him the legs to cover the right side, while Positioning 14 is already notably mature for an 18-year-old. The issue is that the role scores are being held below Strong level by the footballing and defensive contact skills: Tackling 6 and Marking 8 drag down the Full-Back and Wing-Back profiles, while Crossing 6, Dribbling 7 and Technique 7 limit any more progressive wide role.

Out of possession he is more attractive, with Wide Covering Defensive Midfielder and Wide Covering Central Midfielder both scoring 11.67, built on Anticipation 11, Pace 12 and Work Rate 12. Verdict: he is currently a developmental defensive wide runner rather than a finished right-back, but at 18 the engine, pace and positioning give him a credible path if Tackling, Marking and Crossing are trained hard.
